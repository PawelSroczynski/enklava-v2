The problem

Your life runs on someone else's infrastructure.

Power grid, municipal water, supermarket food, the cloud. Convenient — until it isn't. Each is a single point of failure outside your control: outages, prices, policy, distance. The default is to rent your own life-support from systems you can't see.

What it is

Enklava is an ecosystem, not a product.

A method, a set of tools, and a network that take you from “I want to live differently” to a built, resilient home you actually understand and control. The spine is a library of decision matrices that turn vague intent into clear requirements — so you become the person who knows what they want.

The method

Why this isn't another off-grid brand.

“Control your resources” is everyone's pitch. Ours is the how:

  • Matrices over manifestos. Ten decision matrices give you the language and map to define your own requirements.
  • You decide, specialists orchestrate. The expert integrates your requirements — they don't decide for you.
  • Value is in the transition. Not a glossy artefact, but the support, structure and ecosystem trust that get you across.
  • Build at home, share structurally. Proven on real builds, then opened as reusable structure — not bespoke one-offs.
